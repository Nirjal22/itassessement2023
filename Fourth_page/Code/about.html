About US

Owner Of The Club


Nirjal Bista is a name that has become synonymous with entrepreneurship and martial arts in recent years. He is a professional fighter who has won several accolades in various competitions, including the prestigious National Martial Arts Championship. Apart from his success in the martial arts world, Nirjal Bista is also a successful entrepreneur who has founded and runs a fight club dojo. His dojo provides professional training in various martial arts disciplines, including Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, and Boxing. Nirjal Bista's passion for martial arts and his entrepreneurial spirit have led him to create a unique business that combines his two interests. His fight club dojo not only provides top-notch training to aspiring fighters but also serves as a community center where people can come together to learn and practice martial arts. Nirjal Bista's dedication to his craft and his entrepreneurial success have made him a role model for many young people who aspire to be successful in both their personal and professional lives. He continues to inspire others to follow their dreams and to work hard to achieve their goals.
